Getting Started

1. Create a Location

Start by adding where you store things — your garage, attic, closet, or storage unit.

2. Add a Box

Create a box, give it a name, assign it to a location, and add the items inside.

3. Print or Save the QR Label

Every box gets a unique QR code. Save it to your Photos or share it for printing, then stick it on the box.

4. Scan Anytime

Point your camera at a label to instantly see what's inside. Or just search the app.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does CubbyScan require an internet connection?

No. CubbyScan works entirely offline. All your data is stored on your device.

How do I print QR labels?

Open a box, tap the share button on the QR code, and save it to your Photos or send it to any printer via the share sheet.

Can I move items between boxes?

Yes. Edit an item and change its assigned box.

What happens if I delete a location?

All boxes assigned to that location will also be deleted. You'll be asked to confirm before this happens.

What if a QR label gets damaged?

You can reprint the label anytime from the box detail screen. Your data is not stored on the label itself.
